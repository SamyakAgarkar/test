Research Plans

Major research plans for “Novel ALD processes and their applications for interface engineering of mechanical and energy systems”

  • Energy-enhanced ALD equipment and process: Design the tools and investigate the processes of high-energy plasma enhanced ALD, bias-assisted ALD, UV-assisted ALD, etc.
  • Interface engineering of energy conversion and storage devices: Apply novel ALD processes for interface/surface engineering of solid oxide fuel cells, electrostatic capacitors, solid-state batteries, etc.
  • Surface treatment of mechanical components and systems: Explore novel application of ALD process to improve the mechanical properties of surfaces (e.g., tribological, thermal insulation, plasma/wear resistant coatings).
