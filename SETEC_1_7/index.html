Prominent Research

  • HIGHT: A new block cipher suitable for low-resource device, International Workshop on Cryptographic Hardware and Embedded Systems Springer, 2006. (over 800 citations)
  • An efficient trust evaluation scheme for node behavior detection in the internet of things, Wireless Personal Communications, 2017.
  • A privacy preserving authentication scheme for roaming in ubiquitous networks, Cluster Computing, 2017.
  • A reliability comparison method for OSINT validity analysis, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, 2018.
  • Fast implementations of ARX-based lightweight block ciphers (SPARX, CHAM) on 32-bit processor, International Journal of Distributed Sensor Networks, 2019.
  • HANMRE-An authenticated encryption secure against side-channel attacks for noncemisuse and lightweight approaches, Applied Soft Computing, 2020.
  • Blocis: blockchain-based cyber threat intelligence sharing framework for sybil-resistance, Electronics, 2020.

Achievements

  • Over 80 papers published in the SCI(E) indexed journals.
  • Development of ISO/IEC 18033-3 standard block cipher HIGHT.
  • Development of block cipher SEED-192/256.
  • Development of the first high-reliability cryptographic module for Korean nuclear power station APR1400 — Distributed Control System (DCS) and obtaining the KCMVP certification of the developed module.
  • Development of a cyber threat intelligence (CTI) analysis system linked to the domestic incident response system (C-TAS).
  • Development of decryption mechanism for the latest mobile devices and technology transfer to Hancom Inc. (installed in a commercial mobile forensic tool Hancom MD RED).
  • Development of anti-memory forensic response technology.
  • Awards by the National Intelligence Service (NIS), Ministry of Future Creation and Science, Best Paper Awards at numerous international academic conferences.

Research Plans & Government Projects

  • Development of Fast Design and Implementation of Cryptographic Algorithms based on GPU/ASIC: development of a decryption mechanism and high-speed decryption module by analyzing password-based encryption mechanisms applied to unstructured data, encryption apps, and cryptocurrency wallets. Institute for Information & Communication Technology Planning & Evaluation (IITP).
  • Security analysis of block ciphers/hash functions and automation tool development: development of technology to explore vulnerabilities through analysis of block cipher/ hash function’s design principles, structures, and cryptographic characteristics, In addition, development of a tool that can automatically perform security analysis of block ciphers and hash functions. Agency for Defense Development (ADD).
  • AI-based Cryptanalysis: design and development of a machine learning model to analyze the security of symmetric key cryptography algorithms. Ministry of Science and ICT.
  • Development of Forensic Technology on Cloud Service and Energy Cloud Infrastructure: development of digital forensic artifact analysis method, AI-based artifact correlation analysis, and forensic tools for cloud services, cloud-based CPS environment, and infrastructures. National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF).
  • Development of security chip and real-time control protocol security technology for smart factory network infrastructure: development of a TLS/TCP-dedicated security chip for a safe and reliable smart factory network infrastructure, an end-to-end security technology between equipment, gateways and services. Institute for Information & Communication Technology Planning & Evaluation (IITP).
  • Development of Core Technology for Blockchain Platform: research and development of core technologies of the blockchain platforms, enhancement of reliability and vulnerability response of blockchain SW, platform and smart contracts. ITRC Blockchain Platform Research Center.
  • Decryption mechanism and forensic analysis of recent mobile devices: analysis of encryption, key derivation and key management processes of the encryption mechanism in mobile applications, and exploring vulnerabilities to decrypt evidences. Institute for Information & Communication Technology Planning & Evaluation (IITP).
  • Research on Cyber Threats Intelligence (CTI) and Information Sharing Technology: development of cyber threat intelligence analysis method, sharing methodologies for efficient and proactive intrusion response, and integration of CTI with digital forensics. Institute for Information & Communication Technology Planning & Evaluation (IITP).
